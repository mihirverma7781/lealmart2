E-Commerce Stratagies:The drawbacks of ecommerce.

LealMart Pvt. Ltd. in Blogs Jan 09 2021• 3 min read

Viewers are well aware of the benefits of ecommerce to customers, buyers and sellers both. However it would not be wrong to say that the disadvantages of ecommerce are equally damaging. Leaving aside the helpful aspect of the online business, let us find out the drawbacks of ecommerce:

1. Invasion of Privacy- Maintaining privacy in the online world is not as easier as it seems. Unlike Lealmart, many companies and businesses do not have a proper online security portal and even do not abide by their country or any international rules and regulations. Without any adequate online safeguard system, these shady businesses hold sensitive information of many people. Moreover, the information is collected and stored without any knowledge of the owner. That is not recommended by us at all.

2. A Risk Factor- For every anxious online buyer, one question is always in their minds. What if the item is not as per displayed? It won’t be wrong to state this is in fact a risk business. According to a review done about online products by an organization, almost half of the participants agreed to online business being a trap and did not have a good experience with it.

3. Tax Issue and Delivery- For availing services and buying products we generally pay taxes like sales tax, GST etc. These taxes change according to the place one resides. Therefore at times, the tax and delivery charges amount to as much(at times more) than the cost of the product.

4. Technically Challenged Population- Most of the current population is still not comfortable with how the technology works. Transactions without papers are still new to more than 50% of the population. Hence, the tendency to make mistakes is also very high. More often people cannot see the business entity they do transactions with, therefore that creates a swift to physical stores more.

5. Consistency and Online Workforce- Any organization for having a good online appeal, needs to have a bunch of online professionals working day and night to to keep the portals working and appealing. That requires a huge amount of money. Hence many small businesses do not have a strong online portal or presence at all. Therefore that reduces their reach and that keeps them a small business forever.

It is preferable for it includes personal interactivity, items are faster to get, easy return facilities, and moreover people tend to buy from already familiar shops and retailers because of its low risk chances.
